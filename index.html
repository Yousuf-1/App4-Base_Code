Are you an EVOKER or VINDICATOR?

Have you ever been playing Minecraft and thought to yourself "If I were in Minecraft, I wonder what illager I would spawn as?" while getting your home raided? Well you've come to the right place! Answer a few short questions to find out how likely you are to be a Vindicator or Evoker!

Background Information:
In the game Minecraft, an evoker uses magic to summon vexes and evoker fangs and usually spawns in woodland mansions and raids. A vindicator attacks with an iron axe and breaks down doors of homes to get to the player. They also spawn in woodland mansions or raids. Both mobs are hostile to the player.
